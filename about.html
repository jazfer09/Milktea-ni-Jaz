About Us

The feeling of a milk tea shop owner can vary, but common sentiments include excitement, passion for the product, and a drive to succeed in a competitive market. Handling customers involves providing excellent service, understanding their preferences, and creating a welcoming atmosphere. Managing a milk tea shop requires overseeing operations, ensuring quality products, training staff, maintaining cleanliness, and implementing effective marketing strategies to attract and retain customers.
